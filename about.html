Our Mission

We are on a mission to eliminate food waste and ensure everyone has access to affordable, high-quality meals.

Why NavigiFood?

Every year, millions of tons of edible food go to waste while millions of people struggle to afford meals. NavigiFood bridges this gap.

By connecting restaurants, bakeries, and hotels with local communities, we turn potential waste into affordable meals. It's a win-win: businesses recover costs, and families get great food for less.

  • Reduce environmental impact
  • Support local businesses
  • Fight hunger and food insecurity
