Safety Guidelines

  • Food must be handled with gloves and clean utensils.
  • Hot food must be kept above 60°C until pickup.
  • Cold food must be kept below 5°C.
  • Do not donate food that has been exposed to guests for long periods (e.g., opened buffets without sneeze guards).
  • Label all allergens clearly.
  • Donate within 2 hours of preparation for best quality.
